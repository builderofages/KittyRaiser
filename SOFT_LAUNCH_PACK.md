## 2. Play-test questionnaire (Google Form / Typeform)

Keep it short. People will fill out 5 questions, not 20.

1. **What's the first thing you noticed when you spawned?** (open text — catches first-impression flaws)
2. **Did you get bored? If yes, when?** (1-5 scale + open text)
3. **Visuals — be brutal:** ⓐ love it ⓑ rough but fine ⓒ this looks like a 2019 prototype ⓓ unplayably ugly
4. **Would you keep playing tomorrow?** (yes / maybe / no — and why)
5. **One sentence: what's missing for this to be your favorite Roblox game?**

Optional bonus question (catches monetization signal):
6. **Would you spend Robux on a $5-10 cosmetic in this game?** (yes / no / depends what)

---

## 3. What "good" feedback looks like (your decision tree)

After 30+ responses, look at the answers:

| Pattern | Decision |
|---|---|
| 70%+ "would keep playing tomorrow" | Spend $500-1K on first ad batch. Watch retention. |
| 70%+ "visuals are rough but it's fun" | Commission Fiverr cat rig + 5 buildings ($300-800). Republish. Then ads. |
| 50%+ "got bored within 2 min" | Don't spend on ads. Identify which system is missing the hook. Fix. Re-test in 48h. |
| 50%+ "this looks unplayable" | Pause. Visual investment is mandatory before ad spend. Get artist or pivot. |
| 70%+ "would spend Robux on cosmetics" | Build 10 more cosmetic skins immediately. Monetization signal is gold. |

## 4. Where to post
