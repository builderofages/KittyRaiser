# KittyRaiser — Marketing Assets & Listing Copy

## Game Description (paste into Creator Dashboard)

**Cause chaos. Get rich. Stay free.**

KittyRaiser drops you into a 4250×4250 stud NYC where you're a cat with 13 prank powers — Cream Pie, Anvil Drop, Slushie Freeze, Catnip Bomb, Laser Pointer, even Flight at level 100. Build XP, level up, equip premium skins, found a gang, declare war on rival gangs, dodge The Pound when your Wanted stars hit 5/5, and grind the Endless Tower for legendary loot.

**Features:**
- 4250×4250 stud procedural NYC city with brownstones, skyscrapers, taxis, subway, pizza shops, banks, theaters
- 13 unique pranks with real physics (anvil falls, pies splat, fish knockback, ice cube freezes)
- Fallout-style PvP — 15% non-consent damage, mutual hits trigger full PvP
- 5-star Wanted system with Animal Control SWAT response
- The Pound jail with breakout mini-game
- 5 mini-games (Trash Dive, Rooftop Race, Catnip Hunt, Photobomb, Restaurant Heist)
- 75 cat skins, 150 accessories, 100 titles, 30 daily fortunes
- Gangs with treasury and 24h war declarations
- 50-floor Endless Tower
- 8 buyable apartments
- 50+ achievements
- Day/night cycle with weather (rain, storm)
- Mobile + desktop + console controls

**Updates planned:** Underwater, Desert, Winter, Jungle districts.
